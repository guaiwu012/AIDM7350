Sources and references

This page gathers the main references used across the website and organizes them by section. It separates concept-oriented sources, media-and-research sources, and policy/legal sources.

For readability, the references are grouped into three parts: Section 2 for concepts and case materials, Section 3 for research literature, and Section 5 for policy and legal frameworks across Europe, China, and the United States.
